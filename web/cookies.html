Cookie Policy

That Day uses minimal cookies — only what is strictly necessary for the service to function.

What cookies we use

Authentication token (JWT). When you log in on our website (thatday.vip), a JSON Web Token is stored in your browser's localStorage. This lets you stay logged in across page visits. It contains your user ID and email address. It is not a tracking cookie — it expires when you log out or after 30 days of inactivity.

What we do NOT use

  • No advertising cookies
  • No analytics or tracking cookies
  • No third-party cookies
  • No social media cookies
  • No fingerprinting or cross-site tracking

Desktop application

The That Day desktop application stores your authentication token in a local SQLite database on your computer, not in browser cookies. No web cookies are used in the desktop app.

How to manage cookies

Since we only use a functional authentication token, disabling storage in your browser would prevent you from logging in to our web pages. The desktop app does not use browser storage.
